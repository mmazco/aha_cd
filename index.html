about

remember those CDs gathering dust? the wedding video, kids' birthdays, family vacations burned to disc in the 2000s? maybe it's your old music collection with albums that never made it to spotify. those memories are too important to leave sitting in a format nobody can play anymore.

whether it's home movies, photo slideshows, music, or films — if it's on a disc, we can pull it off and turn it into something you can actually watch and share again. we're a local Highland Park service that handles every disc with care and delivers your files digitally organized. simply drop off or we pick up your discs, and we'll have them back to you in days.

services

how it works

  1. pick your package — choose single disc processing ($25) or a bundle package that fits your collection size
  2. we collect your discs — drop off in Highland Park or we'll pick up locally
  3. receive your digital files — get a wetransfer link to download your files

what you get

  • 2-4 day turnaround (not 2-4 weeks like the big box stores)
  • hand-delivered or local pickup (your memories never get shipped off-site)
  • cloud file links, we can also accept USB drives
  • every disc handled with care, cleaned if needed, and files organized properly
